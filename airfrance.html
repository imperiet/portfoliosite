Air France AR World

Lead developer and project manager

A very inspiring project on a very tight deadline.

Air France offers travel experiences all over the world. We had the opportunity to mediate their global reach by developing an app that lets users discover the world, just by taking a few footsteps.

During a couple of days in Gothenburg and Oslo we augmented a square, projecting a worldmap onto the pavement and allowing users to move freely around this space. At different places they had the opportunity to answer quiz questions and potentially even win a flight ticket!
