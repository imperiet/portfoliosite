Nackademin Educator

Educator for the courses "VR UX and UI", "Mobile VR" and "AR"

At Nackademin I taught multiple classes in the field of VR development. Most noteworthy was the course "UX and Interaction design in VR" where I spent a month teaching interaction design, UX for virtual interfaces and real life implementation of it all in Unity. In a field where the rulebook is yet to be written I realized that you need to pay attention to human behavior exceptionally well in order to present your virtual content in a non-intrusive and understandable way.
Other courses included "Mobile VR development" which in a time where Google Cardboard was on the way out and Quest on the way in, was very exciting to both research and teach.
